Animation Specifications for Landing Page

Dimensions:
- Recommended size: 300x300 pixels
- Aspect ratio: 1:1 (square)
- Format: WebM (VP9 codec recommended)

Placement:
- left-animation.webm: Appears on the left side of the central orb
- right-animation.webm: Appears on the right side of the central orb

Behavior:
- Autoplay: Yes
- Loop: Yes
- Muted: Yes (required for autoplay in most browsers)
- Controls: No

Styling:
- Should complement the purple/blue color scheme of the orb
- Should not overpower the central logo
- Should have transparent background where possible
